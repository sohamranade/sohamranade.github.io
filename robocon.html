Project Overview

Robocon is one of Asia's largest robotics competitions, bringing together engineering students from across India to design, build, and compete with autonomous robots. Our team represented BITS Pilani in this prestigious competition, showcasing innovative mechanical design and strategic gameplay.

Competition Challenge

The 2017 Robocon challenge required teams to design robots capable of:

  • Autonomous navigation in a complex arena
  • Precise object manipulation and placement
  • Strategic gameplay against opponent robots
  • Real-time decision making and adaptation
  • Robust performance under competitive pressure

Robot Design and Development

Our team designed and built multiple robots for the competition:

  • Main Robot: Primary competition robot with advanced manipulation capabilities
  • Support Robot: Secondary robot for strategic support and backup
  • Control System: Custom Arduino-based control architecture
  • Mechanical Systems: Innovative gripper and movement mechanisms
Key Innovation: Strategic Design Approach

Instead of focusing solely on technical performance, we developed a strategic approach that considered game theory and opponent analysis. This included designing robots that could adapt to different opponent strategies and exploit their weaknesses while maintaining robust performance.

Technical Implementation

The robots featured several advanced technical components:

  • Mechanical Design: Custom chassis and manipulator arms
  • Control Systems: Arduino-based microcontroller programming
  • Sensor Integration: Multiple sensors for environment perception
  • Power Management: Efficient battery systems for extended operation
  • Communication: Wireless control and monitoring systems

Team Collaboration

Success in Robocon required exceptional teamwork across multiple disciplines:

  • Mechanical Team: Design and fabrication of robot components
  • Electronics Team: Circuit design and sensor integration
  • Programming Team: Control algorithms and automation
  • Strategy Team: Game analysis and tactical planning

Competition Experience

The competition provided invaluable learning experiences:

  • Technical Skills: Advanced robotics and automation techniques
  • Problem Solving: Real-time troubleshooting under pressure
  • Team Management: Coordinating multiple team members effectively
  • Strategic Thinking: Analyzing opponents and adapting strategies
  • Presentation Skills: Explaining technical concepts to judges

Results and Impact

While the competition was highly competitive, our team achieved significant milestones:

  • Successfully qualified for the national competition
  • Demonstrated innovative mechanical design solutions
  • Gained recognition for strategic gameplay approach
  • Established valuable connections with industry professionals
  • Contributed to BITS Pilani's robotics reputation

The experience provided a solid foundation for future robotics projects and helped develop critical engineering skills that would be valuable throughout our careers.
